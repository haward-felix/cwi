Success Stories & Impact

At the Community Wellness Initiative (CWI), we take pride in the positive changes our projects have brought to individuals and communities. Here are some stories and statistics that highlight our success and impact.

Clean Water for All - A Community Transformed

“Before the Amazzi Wellness Project, our children had to walk for miles to find water. Now, clean water is right in our village, and our lives have changed for the better.”

Impact: The project has provided clean water to over 15,000 people across 12 villages, reducing water-borne diseases and improving overall health.

Empowered Farmers through the Financial Freedom Project

“Thanks to the training and financial support, I’ve been able to grow my small piggery business and now provide for my family in ways I never thought possible.”

Impact: Over 100 families have been trained in sustainable farming and entrepreneurship, leading to increased household incomes and food security.

Eco-Tourism and Sustainable Land Use

“Our village is now a model of eco-tourism, and we’ve been able to preserve our forests while creating new opportunities for employment through eco-friendly practices.”

Impact: Through sustainable lumbering and eco-tourism projects, CWI has protected over 1,500 hectares of forest and created sustainable jobs for community members.
